  • Three Card Poker

    Actually two games played with the same three-card hand. Players can play one, or the other, or both! The first option is to bet on your three-card hand against the odds, called a "pairs plus" bet. The second option is to play your three-card hand against the dealer's three-card hand, called the ante bet. The odds and payout at the three-card poker table usually produce the "High Roller" of the evening.

Players in turn roll to "Beat the House!" Special payout for rolling doubles. The easiest game to learn and always well received!
